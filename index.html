#01

Enterprise AI Knowledge System

Designed and shipped a RAG-based knowledge assistant that gives employees instant, cited answers across thousands of internal documents — eliminating the support queue for institutional knowledge.

60%

Support tickets eliminated

3×

Faster knowledge retrieval

2h

Reclaimed per engineer/day

92%

Answer accuracy (human eval)

The Challenge

Knowledge workers were spending 2–3 hours daily grepping through Confluence, Notion, and shared drives. Tribal knowledge lived in Slack threads and people's heads — unretreivable at scale.

The Solution

Built a production RAG pipeline: Rails ingestion workers chunk and embed documents via OpenAI Embeddings, store vectors in pgvector, and retrieve semantically ranked context at query time. GPT-4 synthesizes answers with source citations. Sidekiq handles async re-indexing on document updates.

#02

Legacy Rails Monolith → Scalable Multi-Tenant SaaS

Led a full architectural re-engineering of a Rails monolith buckling under 50k users. Delivered 4× performance headroom and 45% infrastructure savings without a rewrite or downtime.

4×

Throughput improvement

99.9%

Uptime SLA achieved

45%

Infrastructure cost reduction

8s→0.8s

P95 response time

The Challenge

P95 page loads at 4–8 seconds, frequent peak-hour downtime, and a codebase so tightly coupled that any feature change risked cascading failures. Churn was accelerating. The board was asking questions.

The Solution

Executed a full query-plan audit across 200+ endpoints, surfaced 12 critical N+1s and missing indexes. Introduced Redis caching at the model and fragment layers, extracted hot paths into Rails engines, and containerized with Docker for horizontal scaling. Led a 6-person team through a 4-month phased rollout — zero downtime migrations throughout.

#03

Broken Dev Team → High-Velocity Delivery Engine

Embedded as engineering lead into a 15-person team shipping once a month with chronic rollbacks. Rebuilt the delivery system from the ground up — DORA metrics, CI/CD pipeline, sprint discipline, and psychological safety.

Daily

Deployment frequency

3×

Feature throughput increase

70%

Rollback rate reduction

89%

Engineer satisfaction score

The Challenge

Monthly deploys with 30%+ rollback rate. Sprint planning consumed 4+ hours with no clear output. Engineers context-switched across 3–5 parallel tracks, blockers went unresolved for days, and there was zero observability into deployment health.

The Solution

Introduced 2-week Scrum sprints with a hard Definition of Done, backlog grooming ceremonies, and async-first standup format. Built a GitHub Actions CI/CD pipeline with feature flags for safe progressive rollouts. Instrumented Datadog for DORA metric tracking. Ran weekly 1-on-1s and established a blameless incident culture.
